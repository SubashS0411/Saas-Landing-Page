Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel at any time?

Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your dashboard. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period.

What happens if I exceed my usage limits?

We don't shut you off. If you exceed your limit, we'll notify you via email. You'll typically have a grace period to upgrade or we'll charge a small overage fee per request.

Do you offer discounts for open source projects?

Yes! We love open source. If you're maintaining a valid open source project, contact us and we can set you up with a free Growth plan.

Can I switch plans later?

Of course. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Prorated charges or credits will be applied automatically to your next bill.
